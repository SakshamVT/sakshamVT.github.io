AI talking to AI: The Modern Job Application Cycle

A recent trend with applying to jobs has been a tiresome process of applying to hundreds of positions, and maybe only hearing back from a handful. The ability to apply to hundreds of positions, and on the other side, evaluate thousands of applicants for just one position, is something that is made possible by the use of AI. This cycle of AI applications being reviewed by an AI powered system has created an almost unbreakable loop, for both students and recruiters. This post explores the issues with this topic and possible solutions to get out of this loop.
